Reasons It Would Fail
#01
"Good enough" manual process wins
80% use spreadsheets & email. Pain is tolerable.
#02
Attribution is technically unsolvable
Multi-device journeys make individual creator attribution nearly impossible.
#03
Marketplace leakage
Hotels and influencers go direct after first match.
#04
Sojern enters the space
10K+ hotel customers, $120M+ funding. Wins on distribution.
#05
Curation doesn't scale

Reasons It Would Succeed
#01
Perfect market timing

Curation compounds

Attribution = unassailable moat
Proprietary match-to-booking data no one can replicate.

Red-hot M&A market
Intersection of two active acquisition categories.
