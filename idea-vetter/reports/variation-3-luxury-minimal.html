00 — The Idea

A curated marketplace matching vetted travel influencers with boutique hotels

Performance-based compensation: free stays plus commission on bookings. The key bet is that careful audience-property matching produces meaningfully higher conversion than generic platforms.

TargetBoutique hotel marketing directors (5-200 rooms) + mid-tier travel influencers (10K-500K followers)
RevenueSaaS from hotels ($500-$2K/mo) + booking commission (10-15%). Influencers join free.
Exit target$10M — $99M
